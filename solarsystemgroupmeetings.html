Journal Club Meetings

People spend a minute or two saying what they've been doing over the past week. After this one person introduces a recent paper, putting up a few slides/figures giving the background and main results from the paper. The group then fawns over it or tears it to shreds, depending on how much we like it.

Papers are selected by the person listed on the rota. Rules are papers must have been published within the last year. Astro-ph/ArXiv papers are eligible. No self papers. Paper presenters should email the paper link to everyone the Friday before the meeting.

Important: If someone will be away for their slot, arrange a swap with someone and change the online schedule.

Group Roundup Meetings

About every couple of months, everyone prepares some slides/figure or two on something they've been working on the past month, and chats about it for 5-10 minutes to the rest of the group.

No Read Journal Clubs

About every couple of months, everyone picks a paper from Astro-ph/ArXiv based on the title. No reading ahead of time! In the meeting, we each take turns quickly scanning, breaking down our chosen paper, idenitfying key results
